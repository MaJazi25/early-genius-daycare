About Us

Helping children explore, discover, and grow.

Early Genius Learning Academy Inc. is a new childcare center in Ontario. Our goal is to provide a warm, engaging, and educational space for young children to learn and grow through a strong STEM focus.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire curiosity and confidence in young children through daily play and learning activities rooted in STEM.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a welcoming childcare center where children are encouraged to explore, discover, and grow through meaningful interactions and a safe, structured environment.

Why Families Choose Us

We support working families by offering reliable childcare, extended hours, qualified staff, and hands-on learning. Our environment helps children build confidence, social skills, creativity, and problem-solving skills.
